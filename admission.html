Creche and KG Admissions

Our Creche and KG programs cater to young children, providing a safe and engaging environment for their early learning journey.

Admission Requirements
  • Age eligibility for Creche and KG programs.
  • Submission of the child's birth certificate.
  • Health record and immunization details.
How to Apply

To apply for Creche and KG programs, please fill out the online application form and submit the necessary documents. For further assistance, contact our admissions office.

Primary and JHS Admissions

Our Primary and JHS programs focus on academic excellence and holistic development, preparing students for higher education and future challenges.

Admission Requirements
  • Academic transcripts from the previous school attended.
  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher or school official.
  • Completion of the admission application form.
How to Apply

To apply for Primary and JHS programs, please fill out the online application form and submit the necessary documents. For further assistance, contact our admissions office.
